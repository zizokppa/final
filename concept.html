If time is no longer linear

but a circle,

does the future eventually

become the past?

This was how I started developing ideas

from the City as System project:

Turning 8 seconds

into a 3-minute crime scene.

I am deeply fascinated

by abstract expression

and often question our understanding

of time as a 4-dimensional measurement.

We live within measurable boundaries,

yet I am intrigued by the possibility

of stepping beyond this framework

to explore time from a 5-dimensional perspective.
